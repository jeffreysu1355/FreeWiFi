About FreeWifi

Many of us have been in the situation where we have run out of LTE and can't connect online! Well, FreeWifi solves that problem, good samaritans can now host free wifi in different locations, allowing those without LTE to connect online.

All you need to do to use this is create an account and check locations before you leave your house!
